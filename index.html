Phoenix Magic Crest

Unique Skills

  • When you die, you turn into ash instead of dying. User's may revive from this ash as long as it remains undistrubed
    • Destroying ash will result in a full death
    • Revival/Healing magic will not work when in a ash state
  • User can concentrate to purge poison type diseases from within themselves

  • Water Magic (Heavy)
  • Ice Magic (Medium)
  • Gravity Magic (Medium)

  • Fire Magic (Medium)
  • Explosion Magic (Light)

  • Fire

  • Love
  • Life
